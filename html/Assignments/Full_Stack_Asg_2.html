Schools Description
The Kaer Morhen / Wolf School was built in the 11th century.In the days of its glory, the School of the Wolf was one of the most recognized of the witcher schools.Its members gained the reputation of professional and reliable monster slayers.Geralt and Ciri are some of the famous witchers from this school.
The School of the Bear was founded by the first group of witchers that split off from the Order of Witchers.Students must pass the brutal trial of the mountain.Witchers of the Bear School,tend to be loners and wear heavier armor, favoring defense over agility.
School of the Cat is a school of ragtag witchersformed after a student mutiny against their former masters.Cat school witchers as well as their equipment do not deal a lot of raw damage, but can inflict deadly critical hits. One of the notable members is Aiden who was best friend of Lambert from Wolf School.
